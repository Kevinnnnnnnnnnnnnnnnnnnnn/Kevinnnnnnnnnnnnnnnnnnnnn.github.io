Work Cited

 

Basketball is a ball game in which two teams participate

and play on a rectangular basketball court. Each team has

five players, who can pass, throw, bat, roll or transport

the ball in any direction. The purposeis to put the basketball

into the other team's basketball to score and prevent the

other team from gaining the ball control or scoring.

The standard basketball court is 28 meters long and 15 meters wide.

There should be no obstacles at least 2 meters outside the four boundaries.

If it is indoor, the ceiling height should be at least 7 meters. The court

is divided into the center line, the front court and the back court.

The radius of the two semicircles on the center line and the front

and back penalty areas is 1.80 meters. The rectangle under the hoop

is the restricted area, usually called the forbidden area. The area

outside the arched arc in the front and back court is called the 3-point

shooting area, and the shooting outside the arched arc gets 3 points
